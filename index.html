About Me

I am a 22 year old developer from Indianapolis, IN. I have a background in technology prior to my knowledge of programming itself, including but not limited to building and maintaining computers, cellphone repair, as well as a passion for anything with a battery and a button since before I even knew what any of it did.

After many years of playing with technology, I enrolled into a class on javascript at my highschool, Carmel High School, and took the class for 2 full school years. I learned what I thought at the time was a lot of javascript, however, after starting to get into the languages and code involved with making a real website or application, I've come to understand that programming is ever expanding, and I've dedicated my efforts towards understanding and hopefully further devoloping the coding world as we all know it today.
